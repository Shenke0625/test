Loop is a closed loop formed by an infinite cycle between expectations. The time when you are looking forward to something is the most exciting time, but it is also the time when you realize that what you were looking forward to will come to an end. The Ferris wheel records every moment as the most exciting. By moving the Ferris wheel, visitors can experience the cycle of ending or starting something they are look forward to over time.
